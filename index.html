Frequently asked questions

My alarm didn't ring

The most common cause is the app being force-closed. Don't swipe Daybreak away in the app switcher the night before, keep your phone charging, and make sure Notifications are allowed for Daybreak in the Settings app.

Why does the alarm play even when my phone is on silent?

That's intentional. A normal notification stays muted on silent, so Daybreak plays your alarm through the app itself to make sure it wakes you. This is the whole point of the app.

The step counter isn't moving

The steps mission needs Motion & Fitness access. Open the Settings app, find Daybreak, and turn on Motion & Fitness. Note that iPhone counts steps in short bursts, so the number climbs in small jumps rather than one at a time.

How do I delete an alarm?

Swipe left on the alarm and tap Delete, or press and hold it and choose Delete.

Can I use a song from my music library?

Yes. When creating an alarm, tap Wake-up song and pick any track from your Apple Music or iTunes library. You can also choose one of the built-in tones.
